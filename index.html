Pronunciation

Pronunciación

Learn the sounds when com combining a consonant and a vowel. Practice the pronunciation of different syllables. Learn more vocabulary.

Capítulo 1

¡Empecemos!

Ask someone’s name and how someone is. Introduce someone. Say where you and others are from. Give phone numbers, the time, the date, and the day. Spell words and give e-mail addresses.

Capítulo 2

A conocernos

Describe people. Ask someone’s age and birthday. Talk about what you and others like. Describe things.

Capítulo 3

¿Qué te gusta hacer?

Talk about what you and others like to do. Talk about what you want to do. Talk about everyday activities. Say how often you do things.

Capítulo 4

La vida escolar

Say what you have and need. Talk about classes. Talk about plans. Invite someone to do something.

Capítulo 5

En casa con la familia

Describe people and family relationships. Talk about where you and others live. Ask about your responsibilities.
